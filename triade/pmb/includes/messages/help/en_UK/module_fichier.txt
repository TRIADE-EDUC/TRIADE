<h2>Files module</h2>
<p>This module allows you to establish a generic database (address book, ...) based on the custom fields set.
The informations are stored as records.
</p>

<h2>Browse</h2>
Allows to do simple or multi-standard searches on the previously set custom fields and to show the records related to this search.<br />
<h2>Input</h2>
Creation of a new form through custom fields.<br />
<h2>Management</h2>
Management of custom fields, display of the fields, reindexation of the records<br />

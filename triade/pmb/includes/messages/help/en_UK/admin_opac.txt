<h2>Administration of the OPAC</h2>

<p>
The <b>Administration of the OPAC</b> section lets you configure the modules of the OPAC.
<ul>
	<li>
		<b>List of searches :</b> 
		Management of preset searches that people will be able to look up for in the OPAC.
	</li>
	<li>
		<b>Statistics of the OPAC :</b> 
		Save and create statistics about the actions of the users of the OPAC.
	</li>
	<li>
		<b>Browsing by location and by section : </b>
		 Configuration of the virtual browsing by the sorting plans
	</li>
</ul>
</p>
